# Business model
Agni is developed by [Lattice Innovations]((https://www.thelattice.in)), a healthcare technology design and consulting firm. We offer consulting services to, and build software platforms for our Clients.

Client-facing projects take up the majority of our time. We invest what is left in product development—software systems that address common needs. 

Agni is one such endeavor.

Its source code and design documentation is 100% free and open-source. As long as Lattice exists, it will remain free and open-source.

## Implementation services
If you wish you implement Agni in your primary and community care network, feel free to use the information in this website, and the code repositories on GitHub, to deploy it, use it, and modify it. We only ask that you let us know about your implementation, so that we may learn from your experiences.

If you need technical assistance in implementing Agni, we are happy to support field deployment. This is a paid activity.

Infrastructure costs—servers, load balancers, authentication gateways, messaging systems—will be be directly billable to you. There are no hidden markups. Also, this ensures that you are equipped to fulfill the role of a Data Fiduciary, in line with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023.
