## How to enable internal testing
This only applies to Lattice employees.

1. Go to playstore and check if your selected email is correct. (If trying to download with work profile, go to play store in work profile.)
2. Go to `Settings`.
3. Expand `General`, and check if `Developer options` are present. If not, then go to `About`, and click on `Play store version` until there is a snackbar informing you are now a developer.
